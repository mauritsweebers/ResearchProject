PHASE 10 PLAYBOOK – National Transition Guide

This playbook supports policymakers in designing and deploying plant-based transition frameworks. It includes:
- Policy bundles by capacity level
- Timelines for transition staging
- Risk mitigation plans
- Key indicators for monitoring

Templates are scalable by income level, institutional strength, and social acceptance. Ready for integration with SDG, climate, and food security commitments.
