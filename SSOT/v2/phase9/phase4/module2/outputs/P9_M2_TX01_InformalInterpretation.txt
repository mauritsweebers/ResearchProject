
Phase 9 Remediation: Phase 4 - Module 2 (Informal Market Overlay)

This module integrates informal economic dimensions into Phase 4 feasibility assessments.
It provides a structured dataset of food system contributions and labor metrics across key
informal market channels by region.

Fields:
- Region
- Market Channel (Street Vendors, Wet Markets, Informal Cooperatives)
- Food Supply Share (%)
- Labor Share (%)
- Price Differential (%) (vs. formal markets)

Supports more realistic modeling of affordability and labor dynamics in LMICs.
